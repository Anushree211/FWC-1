% ---------------- QUESTION ----------------
\section*{Question 31}

What is the Boolean expression for the output $f$ of the combinational
logic circuit of NOR gates given below?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\begin{center}
\includegraphics[width=0.75\textwidth]{Screenshot_20260220_160911.jpg}
\end{center}

\vspace{0.4cm}

\textbf{Options:}

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $\overline{Q+R}$
\item[(b)] $\overline{P+Q}$
\item[(c)] $\overline{P+R}$
\item[(d)] $\overline{P+Q+R}$
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.6cm}

% ---------------- SOLUTION ----------------
\section*{Solution}

The circuit consists only of NOR gates.

NOR gate identity:

\[
A \downarrow B = \overline{A+B}
\]

After simplifying the given circuit step by step, the final output becomes:

\[
f = \overline{P+Q+R}
\]

\vspace{0.5cm}

\textbf{Correct Option: (d)}

\vspace{0.5cm}

% ---------------- CONCLUSION ----------------
\section*{Conclusion}

Thus, the given circuit behaves as a three-input NOR gate.
