\section*{GATE CS 2010 Question}

\textbf{Question:31}

What is the Boolean expression for the output $f$ of the combinational logic circuit of NOR gates shown below?

\begin{center}
\includegraphics[width=0.9\textwidth]{IMG_20260307_203826.jpg}
\end{center}

\textbf{Options}

\begin{itemize}
\item (a) $\overline{Q+R}$
\item (b) $\overline{P+Q}$
\item (c) $\overline{P+R}$
\item (d) $\overline{P+Q+R}$
\end{itemize}

\section*{Question Analysis}

A NOR gate performs the operation

\[
NOR(A,B)=\overline{A+B}
\]

After simplifying the given circuit we obtain

\[
f = \overline{P+Q}
\]

The simplified logic expression is

\[

After simplifying the circuit

f = \overline{P+Q}
\]
